About Vinegrape Academy

Vinegrape Academy is a leading primary school located in the heart of Ruiru,
KIhunguro. Established with a mission to nurture young minds, we offer a balanced blend of academic excellence, moral integrity, and creative expression.


Our Mission

To provide a safe, caring, and stimulating learning environment where each child is empowered to grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally.


Our Vision

To be a center of excellence producing confident, responsible, and independent learners prepared to thrive in an ever-changing world.


Core Values

Integrity

Academic Excellence

Discipline

Responsibility

Teamwork


Why Choose Us?

At Vinegrape Academy, we blend the Kenyan CBC curriculum with modern teaching techniques to create a fun, interactive, and child-centered learning experience. Our caring teachers and supportive environment ensure each child grows in confidence and character.
